About Us

Welcome to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Swimming Pool

At Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Swimming Pool, our mission is to provide a welcoming and safe environment for swimmers of all ages and skill levels to learn, train, and enjoy the benefits of swimming. We are dedicated to promoting a healthy lifestyle through aquatic activities.

Our History

Established in 2000, our facility has been a cornerstone of the community, offering top-notch swimming training and recreational services. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, continually improving our facilities and expanding our programs to meet the needs of our members.

Our Facilities

  • Olympic-sized pool: Perfect for competitive training and events.
  • Kids' pool: A safe and fun environment for our youngest swimmers.
  • Comfortable seats: Ensuring comfort to all audiance..
  • Modern locker rooms: Equipped with showers and secure storage.

Programs and Classes

  • Beginner Classes: For those new to swimming, focusing on basic skills and water safety.
  • Advanced Training: For competitive swimmers looking to enhance their techniques.
  • Aqua Aerobics: A fun way to stay fit and improve cardiovascular health.
  • Private Lessons: Personalized instruction to meet individual goals.

Meet Our Instructors

Our team of certified and experienced instructors is passionate about teaching and helping each swimmer achieve their best. With a mix of former competitive swimmers and dedicated coaches, our instructors bring a wealth of knowledge and enthusiasm to every class.

Safety and Hygiene

We prioritize the safety and well-being of our members. Our facility is regularly cleaned and maintained to the highest standards. Lifeguards are always on duty during pool hours, and we follow stringent water quality protocols to ensure a safe swimming environment.
